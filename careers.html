Ch. 06 — Careers

Come build calm software
with us.

We're a small, deliberate team in Bengaluru (with some remote folks). We ship weekly, we review each other's work carefully, and we go home on time.

What it's like here

  • Four-day release cycles, Friday is for reading and refactoring.
  • No on-call rotation for engineers below senior level.
  • Annual learning budget of ₹60,000 — books, courses, conferences.
  • Fully covered health insurance for you and your dependents.
  • Hybrid by default. Three days in the office, two wherever you think best.
